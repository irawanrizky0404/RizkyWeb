# Business Requirements Specification (BRS)

# Rizky Irawan Portfolio Website

Version: 1.0

---

# Project Vision

Build a premium personal portfolio that feels like an immersive digital exhibition instead of a conventional portfolio website.

The experience should communicate technical excellence, artistic identity, and storytelling.

---

# Goals

## Primary
- Generate premium freelance clients.
- Attract creative studios and agencies.
- Establish a strong personal brand.

## Secondary
- Showcase multidisciplinary work.
- Present projects through immersive case studies.
- Highlight awards and achievements.
